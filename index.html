About Me

Know more about me

A GIS professional and Researcher with over a decade of experience and a proven track record in delivering innovative solutions across diverse projects in geospatial modeling, urban planning, and environmental management. Proficient in integrating machine learning techniques to enhance spatial analysis and develop innovative solutions for complex geospatial challenges. Adaptable to evolving trends in GIS and AI, consistently delivering impactful projects that address real-world issues and drive positive change.

Currently working as a Researcher at GERS-LEE, Université Gustave Eiffel, Bouguenais, France, involved in MUFFINS project funded by agence nationale de la recherche (ANR), Govt. of France. Previously, I worked as a Researcher at UMRAE, Université Gustave Eiffel and Visiting Researcher (Campus France) at GERS-LEE, Université Gustave Eiffel, France. I have also served as Scientist- Remote Sensing & GIS Applications at Bihar Mausam Sewa Kendra (State Govt. Meteorological Centre), Patna, India.

I hold a Doctorate (Ph.D.) in Geography with specialization in Geoinformatics from Jamia Millia Islamia, New Delhi, India, a Masters in Remote Sensing and GIS from The Global Open University, Nagaland, India, and a PG Diploma in Remote Sensing & GIS from Banaras Hindu University, Varanasi, India. With extensive experience in both research and industry, I bring a unique blend of academic rigor and practical problem-solving skills to every project. Beyond my professional engagements, I am a FIDE-rated chess player, reflecting my strategic thinking and analytical abilities.
